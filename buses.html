Bus Ticket Booking Online - FAQ

Frequently asked questions about bus booking

How can I book bus tickets online?

Booking bus tickets online is very easy. Simply enter your source and destination cities, select your travel date, choose from the list of available buses, pick your seat, and complete the payment. Your e-ticket will be sent to your registered email and mobile number.

Can I cancel my bus ticket online?

Yes, you can cancel your bus ticket online through the MakeMyTrip website or app. Cancellation charges vary depending on the bus operator and the time of cancellation. Free cancellation is available on select buses up to 24 hours before departure.

What types of buses are available for booking?

We offer a wide range of bus types including AC Sleeper, Non-AC Sleeper, AC Seater, Non-AC Seater, Volvo, Mercedes-Benz, Luxury Coaches, Sleeper, Express and more. You can filter buses by type, price, departure time, and amenities like WiFi, charging points, blankets, etc.

How will I get my bus ticket after booking?

After successful booking, you will receive an m-Ticket on your mobile phone and an e-Ticket on your registered email ID. You can show the m-Ticket to the bus conductor while boarding. No physical ticket is required.
